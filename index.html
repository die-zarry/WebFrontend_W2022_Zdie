A wrap up of what the BMI App could be

Tell me how to use your application.

The app is pretty simple: You will be presented with a main page with some inputs, linked images and buttons. The user will enter his age, height and weight.Then press press the Calculate BMI button to calculate his/her BMI. His/her calculated BMI will then be displayed on his screen. The weight is in KG and your height cm as shown in the form. (I wanted to for the user to enter any number and the program will have to convert them into kg and cm automatically, I also wanted the user to specify their gender and age to be processed accordingly. I wanted also to display a specific image in accordance with user BMI whether the user be normal, underweight or obese and to suggest the user with some actions as to doing more exercises, the number calories to enter and out of the body based on his/ner nutrition and also what is necessary for that specific body and how to fix it, eating fruits and vegetables or have more sleep. In fact, I wanted to help the user structure or improve his/her BMI base on the abnormality found. I need to confess that some users are under especial conditions and are limited to some actions. I wanted even to forward them the to some specialist or physicians to help them solve their BMI issue.
